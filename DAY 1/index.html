6. Article Example

Why Semantic HTML Matters

Semantic tags like <header>, <nav>, <main>, <article>, <section>, <aside>, and <footer> describe the meaning of content, not just its appearance. This helps screen readers, search engines, and other developers understand the page structure.

Related Tip

Use only one <main> and ideally only one <h1> per page.
